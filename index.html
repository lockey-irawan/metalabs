Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I join as a research assistant at Meta Labs?
Ma Chung Informatics Engineering students in their 4th semester or above can apply by submitting their CV and academic transcript when the recruitment period opens (usually at the beginning of the semester).

Does Meta Labs facilitate thesis topics?
Yes, we provide various umbrella research topics that students can take for their thesis, especially in the fields of AI and IoT.

Is research here paid or funded?
Most research is funded by internal university grants or external grants (Dikti). Students involved in funded projects have the opportunity to receive incentives.
